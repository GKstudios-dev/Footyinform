📱 Is The Football Post free to use?

Yes! The Football Post is completely free. We believe football news should be accessible to everyone.

We update our news feed regularly. Our goal is to bring you the latest club news, national team updates, and transfer gossip on a daily basis.

No, we currently do not collect or store any personal data from our users. Your privacy is important to us.
