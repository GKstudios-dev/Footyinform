Support

Welcome to The Football Post support page. Find answers to common questions below.

📱 Is The Football Post free to use?

Yes! The Football Post is completely free. We believe football news should be accessible to everyone.

🔄 How often is the news updated?

We update our news feed regularly. Our goal is to bring you the latest club news, national team updates, transfer gossip, and breaking news on a daily basis.

👤 Do I need an account to read articles?

No — every article is free to read without signing in. You only need a free account if you want to like an article or leave a comment.

💬 How do I comment on an article, and what's my username?

Sign up with your email and a password, and pick a username when you create your account. That username — not your email — is what other readers see next to any comment you post.

🔒 Do you collect my personal data?

Reading the app requires nothing from you. If you create an account, we collect your email and chosen username to run comments and likes. We never collect or store your password — that's handled securely by Firebase Authentication (a Google service). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

🗑️ Can I delete my comments or my account?

Yes. You can delete any comment you've posted at any time from within the app or website. To delete your account entirely, email us at gkstudioshq@gmail.com and we'll take care of it.

📧 How can I report a problem or give feedback?

You can reach us at gkstudioshq@gmail.com. We appreciate your feedback and will respond as quickly as possible.

If you have any other questions, feel free to Contact Us.
