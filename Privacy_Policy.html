Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to The Football Post. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Key Point: We currently do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data from our users.

1. Information We Collect

At this time, The Football Post does not collect any personal data. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we collect no personal data, we do not use your information for any purpose. All football news, transfers, and gossip content is fetched directly from our Firebase cloud database and displayed to you.

We use Firebase (a Google service) as our cloud backend to store and deliver news content. Firebase may collect anonymous usage data for analytics, which is governed by Google's own privacy policies. We do not have access to any personal information about you.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect anonymous data to improve performance and user experience. These services include:

  • Firebase (by Google) – for hosting news content

These services have their own privacy policies, which you can review on their websites.

If we decide to add features that collect personal data (such as user accounts, comments, or notifications), we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you before making any changes.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any concerns, you can contact us using the details below.
