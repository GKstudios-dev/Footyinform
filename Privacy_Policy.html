Privacy Policy for The Football Post

Introduction

Welcome to The Football Post! This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app and website hosted on GitHub Pages.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly. Our app and website focus on providing you with the latest football news, including club news, national team news, and transfer gossip.

All the articles are stored in Google Firebase Firestore. This means that when you are online, your device will load the latest posts directly from Firestore, but we do not gather any personal information from you.
