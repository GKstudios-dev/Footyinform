Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I get the latest football news?

The Football Post automatically fetches news from Google Firestore whenever you're connected to the internet. Just open the app and enjoy up-to-the-minute stories.

What if I can’t load any articles?

Please check your internet connection first. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or contacting us for support.

How often is the content updated?

We update news in real-time, so you’ll always have the latest football stories at your fingertips.

If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to us via our Contact Us page.
